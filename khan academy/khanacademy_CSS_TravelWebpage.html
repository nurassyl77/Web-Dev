Travel to Paris

Paris is the city of love and light. The capital of France is known for its elegant architecture, history, and world-famous cuisine. Millions of travelers come here every year to experience the unique atmosphere of this place.
The highest point in Paris is Montmartre Hill. From there, you can see the entire city in the palm of your hand.

Must see places:

  • The Eiffel Tower is the main symbol of Paris.
  • The Louvre Museum is the largest art museum in the world (you can see the Mona Lisa here).
  • Notre-Dame de Paris is the cathedral that inspired Hugo's famous work.

Action plan

  1. Boat trip along the Seine River.
  2. Shopping on the Champs-Élysées.
  3. Enjoying a fresh croissant and coffee from a local bakery.
  4. Go to Disneyland Park and return to your childhood.
