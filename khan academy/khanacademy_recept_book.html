Recipe #1: Classic French Croissant

  • Time: 3 hours
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 500g
Unsalted butter 250g
Sugar 50g
Yeast 10g

Instructions:

Step 1: Mix the flour, sugar, and yeast, then knead the dough and let it rest in a cool place for 1 hour.

Step 2: Place the cold butter inside the dough and fold it several times to create layers.

Step 3: Shape into triangles, roll them up, and bake at 200°C until golden brown.

Source: Classic Paris Bakery Guide

Recipe #2: Homemade Margherita Pizza

  • Time: 45 minute
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza dough 1 pack
Tomato sauce 0.5 cup
Mozzarella cheese 200g
Fresh basil leaves 5-6 leaves

Instructions:

Step 1: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into a large circle.

Step 2: Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough and top with slices of mozzarella cheese.

Step 3: Bake in a preheated oven at 220°C for about 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden.

Step 4: Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves.

Source: Italian Cooking Guide
